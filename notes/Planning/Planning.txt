=== Weapons ===

Blaster
- Fires a 1 damage bullet every 0.4 seconds

Shotgun
- Fires a spread of 6 bullets every 1.6 seconds

Railgun
- Fires a 1 damage bullet every 0.8 seconds
- Hold to charge up to 4 damage
	- 1 damage - 0 seconds
	- 3 damage - 0.6 seconds
	- 4 damage - 1.4 seconds
	- Also increases projectile velocity (1.5, 3, 4) and size (0, 1, 2)
